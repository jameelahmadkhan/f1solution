Specialists

We specialize in the deeper, technically challenging side of software development. A common characteristic of the projects we engage in is the demand for high scalability and innovation of design. Designing, architecting and implementing large-scale Web-based transaction systems and applications is our forte. We specialize in enabling our clients to harness the potential of the Web to expand their horizons.

Integration

We enjoy providing seamless software development capability for our clients, and work hard at building close relationships supported by high levels of integrity, responsibility and trust. Often our clients are themselves software development companies and web designers. We are happy to work behind the scenes to complete the difficult parts of their own projects. Our performance and reliability have made us an extended family of most of our clients and all our growth is based on this trust and recommendations that our existing clients bestow on us.

Commitment

F1 Team is committed to producing work that exceeds our clients' expectations, work that we can feel proud of. Our teams are self motivated and are more than willing to go that extra mile to meet our clients' requirements.
